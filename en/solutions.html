Electrical Discharge Machining

  • Impurity removal:Filters metal debris and contaminants generated during the machining process
  • Maintain clean dielectric fluid:Ensures a stable discharge gap and consistent machining performance
  • Prevent short circuits:Prevents debris from clogging the discharge gap and causing short circuits between the workpiece and electrode
  • High-temperature dust resistance:Durable filter media designed for high-temperature machining environments
  • Extend equipment lifespan:Reduces component wear and improves machining efficiency

Development Focus Area

Working with machine tool manufacturers, we are developing specialized filters for small machine tools in the North American market, and delivering professional filtration solutions for EDM machining fluids.
