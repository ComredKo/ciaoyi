WHO WE ARE

我們是誰

Ciao Yi Co., Ltd. was established in 1981 and is a professional filter manufacturer based in Taiwan, operating with fully integrated in-house production lines and manufacturing equipment.

Founded as an OEM/ODM manufacturer, we have long collaborated with domestic and international customers in product development, specializing in fuel filters, oil filters, air filters, and customized filtration solutions.

By combining a stable supply chain, precise manufacturing technologies, and over 40 years of professional know-how, we effectively support diverse industries and application requirements. Our in-house manufacturing capabilities enable reliable control over product quality, lead times, and mass production feasibility.

Guided by the founding principle of manufacturing excellence and quality-driven operations established by our founder, Mr. Chi, Ciao Yi continues to advance its technologies and processes—delivering reliable, durable, and long-term filtration solutions to customers worldwide.
